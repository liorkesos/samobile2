Young Professionals

Suitable for:

Students, career climbers, and young “up-and-comers” between 22 and 26 years old.


IsraelontheHouse has a new and exclusive trip option which offers you the legendary Taglit-Birthright Israel experience, while enriching your trip with opportunities to network with like-minded people, learn and debate about Israel’s politics, delve deeper into it’s cultural scene, and taste a wide variety of Israeli culinary specialities.

Trip Highlights

  • See and experience the best of Israel from North to South
  • Meetings with successful leaders in Israeli entrepreneurship
  • Culinary tasting tours which include everything from wine, cheese, to chocolate
  • 10 days of jam packed adventure with everything from camel riding to kayaking
  • Philanthropy workshops to help you learn to both earn and give back
  • Free nights out in Tel Aviv and at the exclusive Mamilla Boulevard in Jerusalem
  • Experience traditional Bedouin hospitality and desert culture
  • Dynamic debates and seminars with highly acclaimed speakers on Israeli politics
  • Hidden bonus activities to infuse your trip with that little bit extra

Itinerary

Day 1: Israel Touch Down
  • Welcome to Israel - Land at Ben Gurion Airport in Tel Aviv
  • Opening Ceremony - A toast to your arrival in Ben Shemen Forest
  • Viewpoint - Take in the view and get an "Israel overview"
  • Welcoming Activity - Get the full scoop on the next 10 days
Day 2: A Land of Wine and Chocolate
  • Gamla Hike - Get your hiking shoes on and prepare to explore the Golan Heights
  • Golan Overlook - Take some photos and enjoy the view over Israel’s Northern region
  • Audio Visual Presentation - Listen to war stories & discover the vast history of the Golan
  • Le Chaim - Enjoy a wine tasting tour at one of Israel's top wineries
  • Chocolate Tasting - Satisfy your sweet tooth with tasty visit to the chocolate factory
  • Evening Presentation - Society through the lens by renowned photographer Adi Nes
Day 3: A Taste of Cheese and Kabbalah
  • Travel to Safed - One of Israel’s holy cities and the source of Kabbalah and Jewish mysticism
  • Safed Walking Tour - Stroll through the narrow alleyways and explore the artists colony
  • Meet a Kabbalistic Artist - Be amazed and inspired by the mysteries of the spiritual world
  • Cheese Tasting - Visit the 15th century cheese making factory for a tour and tasting
  • Drive South - Head towards Jerusalem for part two of your Israel adventure
Day 4: Looking Back and Moving Forward
  • Yad Vashem - Tour the Yad Vashem Holocaust Memorial Museum
  • Holocaust Survivor - Meet with a survivor and hear their personal story
  • Mahane Yehuda Market - Taste and explore one of Israel’s biggest open air markets
  • Volunteer Program - Take the opportunity to pack Shabbat meals for the less fortunate
  • Free Night Out - Enjoy a night out in Jerusalems exclusive Mamilla Boulevard
Day 5: Old City Explorations
  • Old City Tour - Explore the alleyways of Jerusalem's Old City and delve into its history
  • Davidson Archeological Center - See and touch the remains of the Second Temple
  • Western Wall - Lay your hands on Israel’s most holy site and experience it’s unique atmosphere
  • Kabbalat Shabbat - Experience a Shabbat service at the Western Wall followed by a delicious dinner
Day 6: Shabbat Shalom Jerusalem Style
  • Israel Museum* - View some of Israel’s most precious historical and modern day treasures
  • Israel Update - Get your head around the conflict with a presentation by Neil Lazarus
  • Free Time - Relax, take a stroll, or meet up with family and friends at your hotel
  • Havdalah - A short ceremony marking the end of Shabbat and the beginning of a new week
    • *Subject to whether your hotel is within walking distance
Day 7: Dead Sea Cruising and Bedouin Cuisine
  • Mt Hertzl - Visit the largest IDF cemetery and memorial site
  • Dead Sea - Soak up the sun, coat yourself in mineral mud, and float away
  • Bedouin Experience - Enjoy a traditional Bedouin feast, music, and an evening under the stars
  • Camel Ride - Explore the Negev with a sunset journey across the dunes
Day 8: Desert Sand and Sunshine
  • Masada - Watch the sunrise and explore the ancient fortress and palace of King Herod
  • Neve Midbar - Relax in the hot springs and waterfalls of this famous desert oasis
  • David Ben Gurion - Visit the home & grave of Israel’s first Prime Minister
  • Cafe Dilemma Discuss Middle East politics and possible solutions
Day 9: Zionism and Southern Flavors
  • Salad Trail - Enjoy an agricultural tasting tour & a look into the "Zionism of the South"
  • Sderot Media Center - Visit a media house which is situated amidst the conflictd
  • Drive to Tel Aviv - Settle into your hotel & prepare for a free night out
Day 10: Raising the Flag
  • Tour Tel Aviv - Learn the history of Israel’s biggest city while touring Jaffa to Newe Tzedek
  • Independence Hall - Get patriotic in the very room where the State of Israel was declared
  • Entrepreneurship Workshop - Meet with a successful Israeli entrepreneur and hear their business success story
